Annual Sagamore Council Terry Beasy Memorial Shoot

Details

Sagamore Council is pleased to share it will be hosting it's annual Terry Beasy Memorial Shoot on Saturday September 12, 2026. Hosted By Sagamore Council, BSA at Camp Buffalo. This event is a sporting clay shoot open to all individuals and teams. All participants who wish to shoot must bring their own shotgun. Shells are provided for the trap shoot but participants in the sporting clays must bring their own shells. Youth 11-18 must be either a registered member of Scouting America or 4-H AND accompanied by a participating adult over the age of 21. No membership requirements for shooters 18 and over. All proceeds benefit the local Scouting programs at Camp Buffalo which provides state of the art shooting complex for local 4-H, law enforcement agencies, Scout units, schools, and other organizations.

Location

Camp Buffalo is located at 9400 N. Boy Scout Road, Monticello, IN 47960. When you arrive on Boy Scout Road, you will see our new pool on the east side of the road. Starting just north of the pool house up to the main entrance of camp is parking along the tree line on the east side of the road. If needed, a lane entrance will be opened just north of the dining hall and south of the main entrance for parking on the west side of Boy Scout Road. For the saftey of everyone, please do not drive on camp property. Violators of this may be asked to leave and not refunded.

Price

There are multiple ways to participate in this event, as such there are different fees depending on how you'd like to participate. Individuals can do the sporting clays for $50. This fee includes the 10 stations 50 bird sporting clays course and the dinner and auction. Individuals can also participate in the trap shoot. This is a 25 shot event for $25. The trap shoot does provide ammunition. Individuals wishing to participate in the dinner and auction only will be charged a $20 fee. Teams can be purchased for $1,000. Team registration includes 5 individuals for the sporting clay shoot outlined before plus a table gun.

Times

Doors are open to the public Saturday, August 22, 2026 from 8 AM to 4 PM

Auction

There will be a silent auction held during this event on Saturday only. Following the silent auction, there will be a brief live auction for specific items. If you have items you wish to put into the auction, please contact event staff as soon as possible. All auction items must be disclosed to event staff on or before August 14th.

Table Gun

Tentatively the table gun will be a Diamondback AR .233 Semi-Automatic rifle. This gun is subject to change due to availability with our arms dealer. Check back here for more information as we get closer to the event.

Accommodations

Camping

Because we are hosting our event on our camp property, free tent camping is available to all participants who wish to use the campsite. Our campsites do not have electricity but bathroom and shower facilites are available at our poolhouse for those who need it. Please note, it is the responsibility of you to bring anything you need for the overnight including your tent. We will have a cafe to purchase food if you'd like.

Cabins

We do have cabins available for use during the weekend. The cost of these cabins are $10. Please note that due to the setup of these cabins, you will have bunkmates. A Scout is curteous so please be curteous to your fellow bunkmates and respect quiet hours.
